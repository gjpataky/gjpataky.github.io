About Me

Hi, my name is Gabriel J. Pataky. I'm currently the Vice President of Loss Mitigation at Fay Servicing, a mortgage servicing division of Fay Financial specializing in the managing of Real Estate Investment Trusts (REITS). Hedge Funds and various other financial companies utilize REITS as a form of investment income. My main role at Fay Servicing is to reduce costs associated with the servicing of REITS to ensure profitability benchmarks are reached. The most common way to acheive a reduction in costs while not foresaking production is to automate the production itself and make the associated tasks less manual in nature. This is where Northwestern Coding Boot Camp comes into play.

At Northwestern Coding Boot Camp I aquired the skills to automate various aspects of the Loss Mitigation process that include application, underwriting, modification caluclation & agreement genreation, all the way through implementation. Fay Servicing's goal is reduce employee related expenses from the current 75% to 60% by the end of 2020.

I have worked nearly 20 years in the financial industry and my education includes a BS in Finance from Arizona State University, a Series 7 & 63 licesing from Charles Schwab, an MBA from North Park University, along with a soon to be a coding certificate from Northwestern's School of Professional Studies.
